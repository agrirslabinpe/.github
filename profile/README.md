### About us:

At AgriRS Lab, we conduct research dedicated to producing science and technology based on remote sensing and geoinformatics, aiming to generate basic and applied knowledge for the agricultural sector. 

The goal of AgriRS is to develop and refine methodologies that effectively promote the sustainable advancement of Brazilian agriculture, contributing to increased productivity and competitiveness.

Additionally, we are committed to training highly qualified Master's and Doctoral students within INPE's Graduate Program in Remote Sensing (PGSER). 

If you would like to learn more about our laboratory and our research, please follow us on social media.
